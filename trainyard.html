How To Play

Drag your mouse across the screen for the trains to follow. Trains will come from start squares and go to goal squares with matching colors. If two trains collide, cross over, or merge, their colors will mix. Going through a paint square will paint the train its color. Going through a scissor square will split the train in two. Going into a rock square or a square with no available tracks will cause your train to crash. You win if all your trains are in goals, with no trains remaining and no trains crashed.
